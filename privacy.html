Alfi Music Player - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Alfi Music Player is designed with privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

  • Music Library Metadata: Song titles, artist names, album information, and artwork from your music library
  • AI Analysis Data: Mood, tempo, energy, and rhythm analysis results generated locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Your settings like accent color and playlist configurations

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers, and no data is transmitted over the internet.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Your music library and preferences never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

Alfi does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Control

You can delete all app data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: amolnw@icloud.com

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available in the app.
